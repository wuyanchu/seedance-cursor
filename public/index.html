What Is Seedance 2.5?

Seedance 2.5 is a multimodal AI video generation model built by ByteDance. It creates 1080p video with native audio from text prompts and creative direction in a single pass.

Text to video

Text to Video with Audio

Describe a scene and Seedance 2.5 generates video with dialogue, sound effects, and ambient audio together. No separate audio editing.

Prompt to motion

Prompt-Guided Motion

Describe movement, pacing, and scene transitions, then let Seedance 2.5 create natural motion from your text direction.

Use text prompts to control style, camera direction, audio mood, and composition across your output.
