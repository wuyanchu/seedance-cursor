What Is Seedance 2.0?

Seedance 2.0 is a multimodal AI video generation model built by ByteDance. It creates 1080p video with native audio from text prompts and creative direction in a single pass.

Text to video

Text to Video with Audio

Describe a scene and Seedance 2.0 generates video with dialogue, sound effects, and ambient audio together. No separate audio editing.

Prompt to motion

Prompt-Guided Motion

Describe movement, pacing, and scene transitions, then let Seedance 2.0 create natural motion from your text direction.

Scene control

Detailed Scene Control
